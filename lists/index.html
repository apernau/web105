Definition/Description Lists

Beverages

Dr. Pepper
Carbonated Soft Drink created in the 1880s in Waco, Texas. The flavors of Dr Pepper are a proprietary mix of 23 flavors.
Redbull Blueberry
A carbonated energy drink with the same base as the original Redbull, only with artifical bluberry flavoring.
Mai Tai
A mai tai consists of two kinds of rum, typically dark and light rum, pineapple juice, orange juice, and a splash of grenadine. This fruity drink delights in taste and embodies beachy, tropical vibes. Great for summer!
Double Chocolately Chip Frappucino
This frappucino is for all my chocolate lovers out there. It includes mocha-flavored sauce, chocolate chips, milk, and ice, all perfectly blended. Comes topped with whipped cream and mocha drizzle for that perfect chocolatey explosion!
Arnold Palmer
A perfect blend of iced tea and lemonade make this beverage the perfect drink for a breezy, summer day. Typically served half and half, but preferences can lean one way or the other. Up to you!
