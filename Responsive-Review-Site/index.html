Raze Craze

Eclectic Food - Family Fun

Welcome to Raze Craze, the epitome of local dining in McHenry, IL.

Nestled on Green Street, Raze Craze is your go-to destination for exquisite, hand-crafted family meals that bring together the heart of our community. At our restaurant, we take pride in delivering a dining experience like no other, with a strong commitment to using locally-sourced ingredients that define the essence of McHenry.

As a family-friendly establishment, Raze Craze caters to all ages and aims to create unforgettable moments around the table. Our diverse menu options offer something for everyone, making us the ideal choice for gatherings with loved ones, whether it's a cozy family dinner or a celebratory feast. Our menu is a delightful fusion of gourmet comfort food from various cuisines, all expertly prepared with a dash of homey warmth. What truly sets Raze Craze apart are our chef specialties, where culinary artistry meets the comfort of homemade dishes. These are the gems of our menu, showcasing the creative talents of our chefs who bring you exclusive flavors you won't find anywhere else in McHenry.

Raze Craze's commitment to using locally-sourced ingredients isn't just a trend; it's a way of life. We believe in supporting local farmers and businesses, ensuring that every dish we serve reflects the freshness and flavor of McHenry.

For a taste of McHenry like no other, dine with us at Raze Craze. It's more than a meal; it's a celebration of community and cuisine.
